About

From Italy to Houston's Insurance Capital

Born and raised in Padova, Italy, I developed an early appreciation for international commerce and cross-cultural communication. After earning second place in a UNESCO cooperative business competition, I knew my ambitions extended beyond borders.

I came to the United States as a cultural exchange participant, then made the decision to stay and pursue a BBA in Risk Management & Insurance at the University of Houston-Downtown, complemented by a Minor in International Business.

Now as a junior, I bring together my European business sensibility with American insurance industry knowledge — fluent in Italian, English, and Spanish — ready to contribute to organizations that value analytical thinking, global perspective, and genuine dedication to protecting what matters most.
